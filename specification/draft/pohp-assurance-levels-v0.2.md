# AAIF PoHP Assurance Levels v0.2 (Draft)

**Status:** Draft  
**Applies to:** HAPP v0.2+

PoHP levels define the **attack resistance** and **verification rigor** for Proof of Human Presence.

- **AAIF-PoHP-1** — Basic presence + explicit approval (low risk)
- **AAIF-PoHP-2** — Verified liveness (passive PAD) + approval
- **AAIF-PoHP-3** — Deepfake-resistant liveness (active/hybrid) + approval
- **AAIF-PoHP-4** — Highest assurance (regulated/high-value), stronger binding, stricter audit and re-certification

HAPP requires that providers advertising PoHP levels provide **certification evidence** (HAPP-PCC) and that relying parties enforce minimum levels per endpoint policy.
